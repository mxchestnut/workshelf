# Chronostem (Fluxborn Subspecies)

## 1. Species Summary
Chronostem Fluxborn embody temporal resonance, formed through exposure to time-distorting fields or ancient arcane anomalies. Their bodies integrate cyclical energy loops, phase-shift nodes, and rhythmic flux patterns that reflect temporal flow.

## 2. Physical Tells & Body Language
Their presence creates subtle time-lag distortions, afterimages, or rhythmic flickering. Movements appear slightly offset or phase-shifted, and their posture is slow yet precise.

## 3. Resonance Summary
Chronostem resonance manipulates micro-scale temporal flow, producing brief slow-time fields, localized acceleration, or phase-delayed effects. Their resonance must remain tightly controlled to avoid destabilization.

## 4. Habitat & Ecology
They inhabit ancient ruins, temporal scars, chronal wells, and areas where time behaves anomalously. They help smooth temporal turbulence and reduce time-fracture risks.
