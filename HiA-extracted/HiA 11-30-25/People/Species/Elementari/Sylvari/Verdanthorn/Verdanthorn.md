# Verdanthorn (Sylvari Subspecies)

## 1. Species Summary
Verdanthorn are defensive-growth Sylvari characterized by bark-plate armor, thorn ridges, and reinforced cellulose layers. Their physiology emphasizes protection, endurance, and physical deterrence.

## 2. Physical Tells & Body Language
They display hardened bark surfaces, thorn projections, and firm, grounded posture. Movements are deliberate, heavy, and protective in nature.
