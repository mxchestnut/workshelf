# Studio Template System

## 🎯 Overview

**Concept**: Transform Studios into AI-powered project workspaces with professional templates.

**Naming**:
- ~~Studios~~ → **Projects** (clearer purpose)
- ~~Articles~~ → **Sections** (more flexible - chapters, modules, parts)
- Add: **Templates** (pre-built project structures with AI prompts)

---

## 📚 Top 10 Project Templates

### 1. 📖 Novel / Fiction Book

**Target Users**: Authors, creative writers, novelists

**Structure**:
```
├── 📝 Premise & Concept
├── 🎭 Characters
│   ├── Protagonist Profile
│   ├── Antagonist Profile
│   └── Supporting Characters
├── 🗺️ World Building
│   ├── Setting & Location
│   ├── Rules & Magic System (if applicable)
│   └── History & Lore
├── 📊 Plot Outline
│   ├── Three-Act Structure
│   ├── Chapter Breakdown
│   └── Key Plot Points
├── 📖 Manuscript
│   ├── Chapter 1
│   ├── Chapter 2
│   └── [Continue...]
└── ✏️ Revision Notes
```

**AI Prompts**:

- **Premise & Concept**
  - "What is the core conflict or question your story explores?"
  - "In one sentence, what makes your story unique?"
  - "What genre(s) does your novel blend, and what tropes will you subvert?"

- **Protagonist Profile**
  - "What does your protagonist want more than anything? What do they actually need?"
  - "What past trauma or experience shaped who they are today?"
  - "What will they refuse to do at the beginning that they'll be forced to do by the end?"

- **World Building**
  - "What's one detail about your world that readers have never seen before?"
  - "How does magic/technology affect daily life for ordinary people?"
  - "What's considered taboo or controversial in this world?"

- **Plot Outline**
  - "What's the inciting incident that throws your protagonist's world into chaos?"
  - "What's the worst thing that could happen to your protagonist at the midpoint?"
  - "How will your protagonist be fundamentally different by the end?"

- **Chapter [X]**
  - "What's the one thing that MUST happen in this chapter for the story to move forward?"
  - "How does this chapter raise the stakes from the previous one?"
  - "End with: What question will make readers turn the page?"

### 2. 💼 Business Plan

**Target Users**: Entrepreneurs, startups, small business owners

**Structure**:
```
├── 📋 Executive Summary
├── 🎯 Company Description
├── 📊 Market Analysis
│   ├── Industry Overview
│   ├── Target Market
│   └── Competitive Analysis
├── 🏢 Organization & Management
├── 🛍️ Products & Services
├── 📈 Marketing & Sales Strategy
├── 💰 Financial Projections
│   ├── Startup Costs
│   ├── Revenue Forecast
│   └── Break-Even Analysis
└── 📎 Appendix
```

**AI Prompts**:

- **Executive Summary**
  - "In 2-3 sentences, what problem does your business solve and for whom?"
  - "What's your unique value proposition that competitors can't easily replicate?"
  - "What are your most impressive metrics or milestones to date?"

- **Market Analysis**
  - "Who is your ideal customer? Describe them in vivid detail (age, income, pain points, behaviors)."
  - "What market trends or shifts make NOW the right time for your business?"
  - "Who are your top 3 competitors, and what's your unfair advantage over each?"

- **Products & Services**
  - "Walk through your customer's journey from problem to solution using your product."
  - "What features are must-haves for MVP vs nice-to-haves for version 2?"
  - "How will your product evolve over the next 12-36 months?"

- **Financial Projections**
  - "What are your top 3 revenue streams and which will generate income fastest?"
  - "What's your customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV)?"
  - "What are your fixed costs vs variable costs, and where can you reduce burn rate?"

### 3. 🎬 Screenplay

**Target Users**: Screenwriters, filmmakers, content creators

**Structure**:
```
├── 📝 Logline & Pitch
├── 🎭 Character Profiles
├── 🌍 World & Setting
├── 📊 Beat Sheet
│   ├── Act 1 - Setup
│   ├── Act 2A - Rising Action
│   ├── Act 2B - Complications
│   └── Act 3 - Resolution
├── 🎬 Screenplay
│   ├── Pages 1-10
│   ├── Pages 11-25
│   ├── Pages 26-50
│   └── [Continue...]
└── 🎨 Production Notes
```

**AI Prompts**:

- **Logline**
  - "Complete this: When [PROTAGONIST] [DOES SOMETHING], they must [OVERCOME OBSTACLE] to [ACHIEVE GOAL]."
  - "What's the ironic twist that makes your concept high-concept?"
  - "Why is this story perfect for the screen vs a novel or play?"

- **Beat Sheet**
  - "Opening Image (p.1): What visual immediately shows us who your protagonist is?"
  - "Midpoint (p.55): What false victory or false defeat flips everything?"
  - "All Is Lost (p.75): What's the lowest point where your hero wants to quit?"

- **Screenplay Pages**
  - "What's the visual hook in the first 10 pages that no one can look away from?"
  - "How can you show (not tell) your protagonist's emotional state?"
  - "What subtext runs beneath this dialogue? What are they NOT saying?"

### 5. 📚 Research Paper / Academic Article

**Target Users**: Researchers, academics, students, scientists

**Structure**:
```
├── 📋 Abstract
├── 🎯 Introduction
│   ├── Background
│   ├── Literature Review
│   └── Research Question/Hypothesis
├── 🔬 Methodology
├── 📊 Results & Findings
├── 💬 Discussion
├── ✅ Conclusion
├── 📖 References
└── 📎 Appendices
```

**AI Prompts**:

- **Abstract**
  - "In 150 words: What did you study, how, and what's the key finding?"
  - "Complete this: This research challenges the assumption that..."
  - "What's the practical implication of your findings?"

- **Literature Review**
  - "What's the gap in existing research that your study fills?"
  - "Which prior study comes closest to yours, and how is yours different?"
  - "What theoretical framework best explains your approach?"

- **Methodology**
  - "Why is your chosen method the most appropriate for this research question?"
  - "What are the limitations of your approach, and how did you mitigate them?"
  - "Could another researcher replicate your study from this description alone?"

- **Discussion**
  - "Do your results support or contradict your hypothesis? Why might that be?"
  - "What alternative explanations exist for your findings?"
  - "What's the most important question your research raises for future study?"

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

### Database Schema Changes

```sql
-- Templates table
CREATE TABLE project_templates (
    id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
    name VARCHAR(255) NOT NULL,
    slug VARCHAR(100) UNIQUE NOT NULL,
    description TEXT,
    category VARCHAR(100), -- 'creative', 'business', 'academic', 'technical'
    icon VARCHAR(50), -- emoji or icon identifier
    is_active BOOLEAN DEFAULT TRUE,
    created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- Template sections (pre-defined structure)
CREATE TABLE template_sections (
    id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
    template_id INTEGER REFERENCES project_templates(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
    title VARCHAR(255) NOT NULL,
    description TEXT,
    order_index INTEGER NOT NULL,
    parent_section_id INTEGER REFERENCES template_sections(id), -- for nested sections
    ai_prompts JSONB, -- array of prompt objects
    created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- User projects (instances of templates)
CREATE TABLE projects (
    id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
    user_id INTEGER REFERENCES users(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
    template_id INTEGER REFERENCES project_templates(id),
    title VARCHAR(255) NOT NULL,
    slug VARCHAR(255) UNIQUE NOT NULL,
    description TEXT,
    is_public BOOLEAN DEFAULT FALSE,
    created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
    updated_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- User sections (populated from template sections)
CREATE TABLE project_sections (
    id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
    project_id INTEGER REFERENCES projects(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
    template_section_id INTEGER REFERENCES template_sections(id),
    title VARCHAR(255) NOT NULL,
    content TEXT, -- Markdown/rich text content
    order_index INTEGER NOT NULL,
    parent_section_id INTEGER REFERENCES project_sections(id),
    completed BOOLEAN DEFAULT FALSE,
    created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
    updated_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);
```

### AI Prompt System

**Prompt Object Structure**:
```json
{
  "prompts": [
    {
      "id": "premise_unique",
      "question": "In one sentence, what makes your story unique?",
      "type": "short_text",
      "context_aware": true,
      "help_text": "Think about genre mash-ups, unique settings, or fresh takes on familiar themes.",
      "examples": [
        "A time-traveling detective solves cold cases by witnessing them firsthand.",
        "Romeo and Juliet retold from the perspective of Juliet's AI assistant."
      ]
    },
    {
      "id": "protagonist_want_need",
      "question": "What does your protagonist want vs what they need?",
      "type": "dual_input",
      "context_aware": true,
      "help_text": "Want = external goal. Need = internal growth.",
      "ai_suggestions": true
    }
  ]
}
```

**Context-Aware Prompts**:
- Reference previous sections the user has completed
- Adapt based on user's answers (e.g., if they said "fantasy" genre, fantasy-specific prompts)
- Progressive disclosure (unlock advanced prompts as they complete basics)

## 🎨 User Experience Flow

### 1. **Create New Project**
```
User clicks "New Project"
    ↓
Browse Template Gallery
  - Featured templates
  - Categories: Creative, Business, Academic, Gaming
  - "Start from Scratch" option
    ↓
Select Template (e.g., "Novel")
    ↓
Customize Project
  - Project Title
  - Optional: Description, Visibility
    ↓
Project Created with Pre-populated Structure
```

### 2. **Working on Sections**
```
Project Dashboard
  ├── Progress: 3/12 sections completed
  ├── Section List (collapsible tree view)
  │   ├── ✅ Premise (completed)
  │   ├── 🔄 Character Profiles (in progress)
  │   └── 📝 Chapter 1 (not started)
  └── Quick Actions
      └── Continue where you left off →
    ↓
Click on Section
    ↓
Section Editor
  ├── AI Prompts Panel (right side)
  │   ├── "What does your protagonist want?"
  │   ├── [Your answer...]
  │   └── "Get AI Suggestions" button
  ├── Rich Text Editor (main area)
  │   └── Full word processor functionality
  └── Navigation
      ├── ← Previous Section
      └── Next Section →
```

### 3. **AI Integration**
```
User clicks prompt → "What does your protagonist want?"
    ↓
Types answer: "To become the greatest chef"
    ↓
Clicks "Get AI Suggestions"
    ↓
AI analyzes:
  - Template context (Novel → Character)
  - Previous answers (premise, genre, setting)
  - Current prompt
    ↓
Returns:
  - "Character Arc Idea: They learn success isn't about awards, but feeding people who need it"
  - "Conflict Suggestion: A rival chef from their past returns as a food critic"
  - "Internal Need: They need to prove their worth to their dismissive family"
```
